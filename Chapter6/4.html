H(P)=−∑x,yP~(x)P(y∣x)logP(y∣x)2 \begin{aligned} H(P) = -\sum_{x,y} \tilde P(x) P(y|x)\log P(y|x) && {2} \end{aligned} ​H(P)=−​x,y​∑​​​P​~​​(x)P(y∣x)logP(y∣x)​​​​​​2​​ 则模型集合C{\Bbb C}C中条件熵H(P)最大的模型称为最大熵模型
