f(i,j)=aiajyiyjK(xi,xj)2 \begin{aligned} f(i, j) = a_ia_jy_iy_jK(x_i,x_j) && {2} \end{aligned} ​f(i,j)=a​i​​a​j​​y​i​​y​j​​K(x​i​​,x​j​​)​​​​​​2​​

L(a1,a2)=∑i=1N∑j=1Nf(i,j)−∑i=1Nai3 \begin{aligned} L(a_1, a_2) = \sum_{i=1}^N\sum_{j=1}^Nf(i,j) - \sum_{i=1}^Na_i && {3} \end{aligned} ​L(a​1​​,a​2​​)=​i=1​∑​N​​​j=1​∑​N​​f(i,j)−​i=1​∑​N​​a​i​​​​​​​​3​​

在公式（3）中，将a1a_1a​1​​、a2a_2a​2​​是变量，其它参数a是常量，把公式（3）分成包含变量的部分和常量部分：
L(a1,a2)=f(1,1)+f(1,2)+∑j=3Nf(1,j)+f(2,1)+f(2,2)+∑j=3Nf(2,j)+∑i=3Nf(i,1)+∑i=3Nf(i,2)++∑i=3N∑j=3Nf(i,j)−a1−a2−∑i=3Nai4 \begin{aligned} L(a_1, a_2) = f(1,1)+f(1,2)+\sum_{j=3}^Nf(1,j) \\ +f(2,1)+f(2,2)+\sum_{j=3}^Nf(2,j) \\ +\sum_{i=3}^Nf(i,1)+\sum_{i=3}^Nf(i,2) + +\sum_{i=3}^N\sum_{j=3}^Nf(i,j) \\ -a_1 - a_2 - \sum_{i=3}^Na_i && {4} \end{aligned} ​L(a​1​​,a​2​​)=f(1,1)+f(1,2)+​j=3​∑​N​​f(1,j)​+f(2,1)+f(2,2)+​j=3​∑​N​​f(2,j)​+​i=3​∑​N​​f(i,1)+​i=3​∑​N​​f(i,2)++​i=3​∑​N​​​j=3​∑​N​​f(i,j)​−a​1​​−a​2​​−​i=3​∑​N​​a​i​​​​​​​​4​​
